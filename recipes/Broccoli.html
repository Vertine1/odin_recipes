Oven Fried Chicken

I love fried chicken, but it takes so long and is such a big mess. I came up with this oven-fried version, and have been making it ever since! Everyone I have given the recipe to has loved it as well.

ingredients

  • ½ pound bacon
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ¾ cup sliced almonds
  • ¾ cup raisins
  • 1 small red onion, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 heads fresh broccoli, cut into bite-sized pieces

Step By step Guide on How to Cook

  1. Make the dressing.
  2. Cook and crumble the bacon.
  3. Combine the ingredients.
